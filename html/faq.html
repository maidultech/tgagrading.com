Frequently Asked Questions

"Got questions? Check out the answers to our most common inquiries below. If you need further help, feel free to reach out!"

Card grading is the process of evaluating the condition and authenticity of a card. Graded cards are given a score based on various factors, such as corners, edges, centering, and surface condition. Grading adds value to cards as it ensures authenticity and preservation of the card's quality.

At TGA, we use a meticulous process that involves multiple steps to ensure accuracy. Your card goes through thorough evaluation by experts who inspect for authenticity, wear and tear, centering, and more. Each card is then assigned a grade and encapsulated in a protective slab.

We offer different grading tiers based on the turnaround time and the value of the card. - Standard Grading: $20 per card (10 business days) - Express Grading: $40 per card (5 business days) - Premium Grading: $60 per card (2 business days)

Our standard grading process takes around 10 business days. However, we also offer express and premium grading services for faster turnaround times.

Yes, you will receive a tracking number once we receive your card, and you can monitor the grading process through your TGA account dashboard.

We grade a variety of cards, including trading cards such as Pokémon, sports cards, Magic: The Gathering, and Yu-Gi-Oh!. If you're unsure whether your card qualifies, feel free to contact us for more details.

Submitting your cards is easy! Simply fill out our online submission form, pack your cards securely, and send them to the provided address. We’ll take care of the rest.

We take utmost care when handling your cards. In the unlikely event of damage, we have insurance policies in place to compensate for any loss during the grading process.

Our grading criteria are based on four key factors: centering, edges, corners, and surface condition. Each card is thoroughly inspected by our experts to ensure fair and accurate grading.
