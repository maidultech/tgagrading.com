ABOUT US

True Grade Authentication is leading edge grading authentication company.

We provide our customers with a true grading experience where they can have their cards graded at an affordable price with quick turnaround times.

TGA offers members:

  • Grading and Authentication of their sport and TCG cards
  • Quick Turnaround times
  • Customer Service via email and phone
  • Seamless and transparent status of their submission

True Grade Authentication is a registered corporation in the province of British Columbia.

Professional Sports Authenticator (TAG) & TAG/DNA are divisions of Collectors Holdings, Inc.
